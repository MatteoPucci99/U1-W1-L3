Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart craberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons istant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons istant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

    • Method

    1. Make the focaccia dough

      2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, thoung I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
